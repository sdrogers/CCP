Welcome to the website for the 5th edition of the Cottier Chamber Project.

Latest News...

Tickets for the 2015 festival are now on sale either online or by phone. We will be opening a box office in the Hillhead Subway branch of Tinderbox from the 2nd May onwards, so if you would like to buy your tickets in person, stop in for a coffee!

If you have either a Subway card or a myWaitrose card then you will be able to get £1 off your booking - don’t forget to ask.

There are also plenty of opportunities to volunteer with us. We are on the lookout for stewards, first-aiders, hosts for visiting artists, bloggers, home bakers, brochure or flyer deliverers and people to host rehearsals!


Composition competition

Our composition competition for 2015 is for a work of 5-7 minutes in length for 3-5 string players. There is a £500 prize for the work, with performances in Glasgow and Edinburgh, and a workshop with players from the Royal Scottish National Orchestra for shortlisted pieces.
