Welcome to the website for The Cottier Chamber Project. The programme for the 2016 edition of the festival (3rd - 24th June) will be launched at the end of February. In the meantime, you can browse through the 2015 programme, read reviews and look at our photo galleries by using the links on the left. If you would like to find out more about getting involved in the festival, please get in touch via our Contact page.

Latest News...

We’re delighted to have been awarded the EFFE Label by the European Festivals Association! This new accreditation adds us to the list of Europe’s finest festivals and was awarded following a meeting of the EFA’s panel in May. We are looking forward to building connections with festivals and venues across Europe! Read the press release here.

Composition Competition: Congratulations to Claire McCue, whose string quartet, Beauty and the Beastie won this year’s competition. The RSNO ensemble will première it on the opening night.

Tickets for the 2015 festival are now on sale either online or by phone. We will be opening a box office in the Hillhead Subway branch of Tinderbox from the 2nd May onwards, so if you would like to buy your tickets in person, stop in for a coffee!

If you have either a Subway card or a myWaitrose card then you will be able to get £1 off your booking - don’t forget to ask.

There are also plenty of opportunities to volunteer with us. We are on the lookout for stewards, first-aiders, hosts for visiting artists, bloggers, home bakers, brochure or flyer deliverers and people to host rehearsals!
