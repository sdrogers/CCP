Tickets

Online and telephone

You can book tickets securely online for most of the performances here. You can print tickets at home, or bring an electronic copy of your confirmation with you. Telephone bookings can be made on 0141 357 4000. The box office line is staffed at different times, depending on the performance schedule. If there is no answer, please leave a message on the answer phone and you will receive a call back.

In person booking

Tickets can be bought in person at Currie and Quirk Opticians, on Byres Road. This will be possible between 10am and 4.45pm, Monday to Saturday starting Wednesday 25th May.

Tickets for future programmes can also be purchased at any of the performances.

Please note that tickets for the Grosvenor Cinema screenings can only be purchased from the Cinema Box Office.

Seating is unreserved.

You can collect tickets from the box office on the night of the performance. We’re always open at least an hour before the start of the show.

Loyalty Card

Don't forget to get your loyalty card stamped at each performance. Your sixth visit will be free! Please note that this will not apply to the last two events, held at Tramway.
