Support Us

The Cottier Chamber Project is run by a very small team, so if you would like to be involved with helping out, either financially or practically, then they would be delighted to hear from you!

To help financially:

Our funding comes from several sources – we apply for grants from a number of funding bodies, we arrange sponsorship for the series, and we also welcome individual donations.

If you would like to be involved as a sponsor, please contact us through the Contact page.

If you would like to make a donation as an individual, you can either send a cheque or donate via PayPal. If you are a UK tax payer, please consider using Gift Aid to maximise your donation (you can download a form here or contact us to request one in the post). To help practically:

There are many ways to help out practically

  • volunteer to help with stewarding, setting up and clearing away, brochure delivery
  • loan of instruments (if you have a wonderful grand piano or harpsichord that doesn’t get much use…) or props (Persian rugs and standard lamps are welcome!)
  • provide accommodation for visiting artists
  • provide rehearsal space for groups in the weeks leading up to the series
  • bring down some home baking to keep the musicians going!
  • spread the word about the series and invite your friends to concerts
    • If this looks as though it might suit you, please consider joining our friends group – The Cottier Chamber Circle. Download information here.
